Rahel Amare Adult Foster Home

Our Foster Home is located at 8870 SE Manfiled Court clackamas. It is a quiet nighborhood where no noises can be heard. The location of the house is highly convinent for public transportations and Trimet lift. Our Foster Home is one level house and very spacious for wheelchair. There are 5 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms with rollin showers,living room and dinning room. Every room has it own Tv and internet setted up.

Caregivers and provider are experianced in working with people with devlopmental disabilities, medical complication, behavioral problems,autism and many more. They are also trained and certified with First Aid, CPR, Oregon intervation systems, fire and emergency preparation, basics of blood pressure, preventing abuse in care home, caregiver tips to support individuals, managing constipation, lifting and moving wheelchair people, dimentia, mental health, diabetic, managing sleep problems, and so on .

In general, it is a house where everyone is welcomed and well cared for without descrimination and with respect.
