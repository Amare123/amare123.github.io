Rahel Amare Adult Foster Home

Our Foster Home is located at 8870 SE Manfiled Court clackamas. It is a quiet nighborhood where no noises can be heard. The location of the house is highly convinent for public transportations and Trimet lift. Our Foster Home is one level house and very spacious for wheelchair. There are 5 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms with rollin showers,living room and dinning room. Every room has it own Tv and internet setted up. Caregivers and provider are present 24/7 and they are certified with several trainings such as First Aid, CPR, Oregon Intervantion System and others. In general, it is a house where everyone is welcomed and well cared for without descrimination and with respect.
